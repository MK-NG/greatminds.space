A creative conundrum

The world is more uncertain and volatile. Businesses can no longer rely on an expert team of “creatives” - a thriving business needs to empower everyone to creatively solve problems.

Creative thinking courses don’t change peoples’ behaviour. Courses mostly focus on tools and techniques, but a creative thinking toolkit is no use if you don’t have the confidence to practice it.

How we can help

Great Minds is designed around the study of how humans form habits. We give you and your teams the tools and space to help learn, practice and develop creative thinking.

Stack your habits

We build on your existing habits by nudging you to practice at a time of day that's convenient and repeatable.

Reduce mental load

We give you time based exercises so that they fit into your day - no matter how busy you are.

Use your why

We remind you of your motivations for improving your creative thinking when things get hard

Celebrate your wins

We celebrate milestones no matter how small, as over time doing something is better than doing nothing

Choose what you do

We give you the option to pick exercises that appeal to you so that you are the master of your own learning.

Find accountablity

Connect with other like-minded individuals to help each other achieve your goals
