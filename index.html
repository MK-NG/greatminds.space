The problem

People say “I’m not creative.”

Creativity isn’t a trait, it’s a mindset.

You build creativity through experience and over time that experience becomes a behaviour.

You then use these behaviours in life; when you are solving a problem, in work situations, with your kids.

Once you have unlocked your creativity the applications of it are endless.
