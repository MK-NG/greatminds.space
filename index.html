A creative conundrum

Creative thinking is the key to growth and survival in businesses. Businesses are recognising this, but to solve the deficit of creative thinkers in their organisations they either rely on a crack team of “creatives” to solve all these problems for them or they send a handful of people on a training course and expect them to come back with endless great ideas. This model doesn't work. It’s expensive, distracting and doesn't deliver long term results.

To have creative confidence, you need to practice being creative. How often have you heard your team say “I’m not creative.” That’s the root of the problem - creativity isn’t a trait, it’s a mindset. You build it through experience and over time it becomes a behaviour. You can then lean on that behaviour when you are solving a problem, or being open to new opportunities.
